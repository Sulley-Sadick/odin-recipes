Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a type of baked pasta from Italy made from flat sheets of egg pasta layered with various sauces such as meat ragù, béchamel sauce and/or various vegetables and various types of cheese

Ingredients

  • Meat:Ground beef, Italian sausage, or meat ragu
  • Sauce:Spaghetti sauce, red wine, canned crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, thyme, and bay leaves.
  • Cheese:Mozzarella, ricotta, Parmesan, or pecorino
  • Eggs:Helps bind the ricotta mixture and keeps it from becoming grainy.
  • vegetables:Onion, garlic, carrots, and celery.
  • Herbs:Basil

Steps

  1. To make lasagna, you can layer pasta sheets, sauce and cheese in an oven-proof dish.
  2. You can use low-moisture mozzarella because it melts evenly.
  3. You can use cottage cheese instead of ricotta because it retains its creamy consistency better.
  4. You can use a 9 x 13-inch ceramic or enameled cast-iron baking pan.
